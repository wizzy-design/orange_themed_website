About Us

Covenant University is a growing, dynamic vision-driven University, founded on a Christian mission ethos and committed to pioneering excellence at the cutting edge of learning. We run with a compelling vision of raising a new generation of leaders for the African continent. We are doing this on a holistic, human development and integrated learning curriculum to raise total Men and Women who will develop their world. Our Core-Values of Spirituality, Possibility Mentality, Capacity Building, Integrity, Responsibility, Diligence, and Sacrifice are what defines our commitment to excellence..
